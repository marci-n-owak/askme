About

AskMe is the easiest way to improve your foreign language vocabulary. With an easy creation process you can create a custom list of words for your own needs. You can also test yourself with lists made by other users. Use it to get a good mark on a test, or just to make progress. Nothing is stopping you from becoming fluent in the language of your choice.
